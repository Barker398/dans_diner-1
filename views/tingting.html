The Best Tiramisu You will Ever Make

Tiramisu is a coffee-flavoured Italian dessert. It is made of ladyfingers dipped in coffee, layered with a whipped mixture of eggs, sugar, and mascarpone cheese, flavoured with cocoa. The recipe has been adapted into many varieties of cakes and other desserts.

Ingredients

  • 4 large egg yolks
    • +
  • ½ cup/100 grams granulated sugar, divided
    • +
  • 1 cup/227 grams mascarpone (8 ounces)
    • +
  • 2 tablespoons unsweetened cocoa powder
    • +
  • About 24 ladyfingers (from one 7-ounce/200-gram package)

    • Directions

  • Step 1: Quickly dip half of the ladyfingers in coffee syrup and arrange in a 9×13 casserole.
  • Step 2: Combine 6 yolks and 3/4 cup sugar and continue whisking over a saucepan of simmering water for 9-10 minutes.
  • Step 3: Beat 16 oz mascarpone with 3 Tbsp rum then beat in the warm yolk mixture until blended.
  • Step 4: Whip 2 cups cold heavy cream to medium peaks. With a spatula, fold into the mascarpone mixture until blended.
  • Step 5: Quickly dip each ladyfinger into the espresso mixture. Dust top layer with remaining tablespoon of cocoa powder.
