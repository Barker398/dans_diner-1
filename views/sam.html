BBQ Bacon Cheeseburger Sliders

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound of ground beef
  • 1/3 cup bacon chopped
  • 1/3 cup sharp cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 cup BBQ sauce
  • 3/4 cup of french fried onions
  • pickles
  • 6 slider buns

    • directions:

  • Step 1: Pre heat grill to 400 degrees, and clean.
  • Step 2: Spray grill with non stick cooking spray.
  • Step 3: In a large bowl, combine ground beef, bacon, sharp cheddar cheese, and 2 Tbsp of bbq sauce, and mix thoroughly.
  • Step 4: Divide into 6 portions.
  • Step 5: Place patties on the grill and cook covered for 8-10 minutes, turning at least once until the internal temp reaches 140 degrees for medium doneness.
  • Step 6: To serve, put your slider on the bun and add bbq sauce, french fried onions, and pickles.
