    • Red, White and Blueberry Pie

    This refreshing, patriotic dessert is a great way to top off one of our delicious meals of the day this summer!

    Total prep time: 20 minutes + chilling and makes 8 servings.

    Ingredients

  • 2 ounces white baking chocolate, melted
  • 1 graham cracker crust (9 inches)
  • 3/4 cup sliced fresh strawberries
  • 1 package (8 ounces) cream cheese, softened
  • 3/4 cup confectioners' sugar
  • 3/4 cup 2% milk
  • 1 package (3.3 ounces) instant white chocolate pudding mix
  • 1 cup whipped topping
  • 8 fresh strawberries, halved lengthwise
  • 1 cup fresh blueberries

    • directions

  • Step 1: Spread melted chocolate onto bottom and sides of crust. Arrange sliced strawberries over chocolate.
  • Step 2: In a bowl, beat cream cheese and confectioners' sugar until smooth; gradually beat in milk. Add pudding mix; beat on low speed until thickened, about 2 minutes. Spread over strawberries.
  • Step 3: Decorate pie with whipped topping, blueberries and halved strawberries. Refrigerate until serving.
